## Brand Voice & Tone

### Personality Attributes
- **Professional**: Expert, precise, reliable
- **Premium**: High-quality, sophisticated, refined
- **Approachable**: Clear, helpful, friendly
- **Confident**: Assured, authoritative, trustworthy

### Voice Characteristics
- **Clarity First**: Write in simple, direct language
- **Benefit-Oriented**: Focus on client outcomes
- **Emotionally Balanced**: Professional yet warm
- **Detail-Conscious**: Show precision without overwhelming

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice and strong verbs
- Keep sentences concise (15-20 words average)
- Avoid industry jargon unless explained
- Use "you" and "your" to speak directly to clients
- Emphasize results and benefits over features

### Content Principles
- **5-Second Rule**: Core value proposition understood in 5 seconds
- **Scannable**: Use headers, lists, and short paragraphs
- **Trust-Building**: Include specifics, evidence, and clear promises

## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Logo Variants
- **Primary Logo**: Full color version for light backgrounds
- **Light Logo**: White/off-white version for dark backgrounds
- **Icon Only**: Symbol version for tight spaces

### Minimum Size
- **Digital**: 120px width minimum
- **Print**: 0.5 inches (12.7mm) width minimum
- **Social Media**: 180x180px minimum

### Clear Space
- **Minimum**: 0.5x logo height on all sides
- **Preferred**: 1x logo height on all sides

### Placement Rules
- **Top Left**: Preferred for header placement
- **Center**: Acceptable for hero sections
- **Right**: Acceptable with sufficient spacing

### Logo Don'ts
- ‚ùå Don't stretch, distort, or alter proportions
- ‚ùå Don't change colors (except for approved variants)
- ‚ùå Don't add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
- ‚ùå Don't rotate the logo (except for design-specific cases)
- ‚ùå Don't place logo on busy backgrounds that reduce readability

### Background Requirements
- **High Contrast**: Logo must have minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- **Solid Colors**: Preferred over patterns or gradients
- **Safe Zones**: Avoid placing near edges or competing elements

## Spacing & Layout Rules

### Spacing Scale
- **Micro**: 4px (8px) - Small gaps, icon spacing
- **Small**: 8px (16px) - Element padding, tight spacing
- **Medium**: 16px (32px) - Standard spacing, margins
- **Large**: 24px (48px) - Section spacing, breathing room
- **XLarge**: 32px (64px) - Major section breaks
- **XXLarge**: 48px (96px) - Hero sections, major separations

### Grid System
- **12-column grid** for desktop layouts
- **8-column grid** for tablet layouts
- **4-column grid** for mobile layouts
- **Max Width**: 1200px for content containers

### Component Spacing
- **Cards**: 24px internal padding, 16px external margins
- **Sections**: 64px - 96px vertical padding
- **Buttons**: 16px horizontal padding, 8px vertical padding
- **Forms**: 24px between form fields

## Brand Misuse Guidelines

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- **Inconsistent Colors**: Stick to the defined color palette
- **Font Mixing**: Use only Inter as the primary typeface
- **Inconsistent Spacing**: Follow the spacing scale
- **Logo Abuse**: Don't overuse or incorrectly place logos
- **Background Conflicts**: Ensure text readability

### Quality Standards
- **Image Quality**: Use high-resolution, professional photography
- **Alignment**: Maintain consistent alignment and grids
- **Contrast**: Ensure WCAG AA compliance (4.5:1 contrast)
- **Consistency**: Apply brand rules consistently across all touchpoints

### Brand Guardianship
- Review all external materials before publishing
- Maintain brand asset library with approved resources
- Regular brand audits to ensure consistency
- Training for team members on brand guidelines
